About

I am a recent Master's graduate of West Virginia University. Despite my focus on data processing and visualization, I consider myself a jack of all trades, coding in Java, Python, C++, Android, and for the Web.

In 2013, I began leading a small team of undergraduates in building a visualization CAVE for studying full resolution neurons created with ssBFEM.

Current Interests: android, minimalist design, building full stack products, simple living, personal stat tracking, guitar, and dogs.
